About me

I’m a Rails engineer and recent graduate of The Iron Yard in Indianapolis. I graduated from DePauw University in 2013 with a degree in music and French.

I spent the academic year after I graduated in France, teaching English to middle schoolers in a small town near Paris. That experience was amazing and invaluable in so many ways, but it confirmed that teaching just wasn’t for me. A friend there suggested that I try coding, knowing that I have a penchant for language learning and that I spend an awful lot of time on my computer anyway.

A year and a half later I decided to go beyond self-teaching in my spare time and totally immerse myself in code at The Iron Yard. There I gained more than a solid foundation in Ruby on Rails, HTML/CSS and Bootstrap, git and GitHub, Javascript and jQuery, SQL, RESTful APIs, and Heroku deployment; I learned how to think and solve problems like a developer and armed myself with the tools to keep learning.

I’m so excited to start a career as a developer and continue to learn and challenge myself while building useful applications.
